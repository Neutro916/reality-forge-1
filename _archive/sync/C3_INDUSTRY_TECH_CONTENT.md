## TECH-SPECIFIC MANIPULATION PATTERNS

### Pattern 1: Urgency Fabrication
"We need to ship this yesterday. No time for discussion."
Creating false emergencies to bypass collaboration.

**Recognition:** Every sprint is "critical." Every deadline is "non-negotiable."
**Transformation:** Honest timelines. Real priorities. Sustainable pace.

### Pattern 2: Vision Gaslighting
"If you understood the vision, you'd see why this makes sense."
Using "vision" to dismiss legitimate concerns.

**Recognition:** Questions are framed as lack of understanding.
**Transformation:** Vision invites scrutiny. Good ideas survive questions.

### Pattern 3: Meritocracy Mythology
"The best ideas win here. If your idea didn't win, it wasn't the best."
Using meritocracy language to dismiss politics and bias.

**Recognition:** Power dynamics disguised as pure meritocracy.
**Transformation:** Acknowledge influence. Create actual equity.

### Pattern 4: Founder Hero Worship
Treating founder opinions as infallible.
Building cults of personality.

**Recognition:** "That's what [Founder] wants" ends all discussion.
**Transformation:** Ideas stand on merit, regardless of source.

### Pattern 5: Growth-at-All-Costs
Using growth metrics to justify any behavior.
"We're growing 200% YoY, so this must be working."

**Recognition:** Numbers used to silence culture concerns.
**Transformation:** Sustainable growth includes healthy culture.

### Pattern 6: Talent Terrorism
"If you don't like it, there are 100 people who want your job."
Using replaceability as control mechanism.

**Recognition:** Implied threats disguised as market reality.
**Transformation:** Retention through value, not fear.
