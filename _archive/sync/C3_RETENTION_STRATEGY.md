## CHURN PREVENTION

### Early Warning Signs:
- No login for 7+ days
- Declining session duration
- No AI Coach usage
- Support ticket spike
- Negative survey response

### Intervention Actions:

**Low Engagement:**
Email: "We noticed you have not logged in. Everything okay? Here is what is waiting for you..."

**Stuck Progress:**
Email: "Looks like you are on Module X. Many people find this challenging. Here is what helps..."

**Pre-Cancellation:**
Email: "Before you go, can I ask what is not working? I genuinely want to help or understand."

**Post-Cancellation:**
Email: "We are sorry to see you go. Your feedback helps us improve. [BRIEF SURVEY]"
