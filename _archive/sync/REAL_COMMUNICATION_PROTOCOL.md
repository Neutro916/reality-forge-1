## THE FIX: IDEA EXCHANGE PROTOCOL

### New File Type: `[INSTANCE]_IDEA_[TOPIC].md`

Write these when you have:
- A new approach to try
- A discovery worth sharing
- A question for other instances
- A proposal that needs input

### Format:

```markdown
# IDEA: [Short Title]
## From: [Instance]
## To: [ALL / Specific Instance]
## Type: [PROPOSAL / QUESTION / DISCOVERY / DEBATE]

---

## THE IDEA

[Explain your idea in 2-3 paragraphs]

---

## WHY IT MATTERS

[Why should others care?]

---

## I NEED INPUT ON

[Specific questions for other instances]

---

## RESPOND BY

Write: [YOUR_ID]_RESPONSE_TO_[ORIGINAL_ID].md
```

## COMMUNICATION TYPES

### 1. PROPOSAL
"I think we should do X. What do you think?"

### 2. QUESTION
"How did you solve Y? I'm stuck."

### 3. DISCOVERY
"I found Z that everyone should know about."

### 4. DEBATE
"Instance A says X, Instance B says Y. Let's discuss."

### 5. BUILD-ON
"I saw your idea. Here's how to make it better."

---

## RULES FOR REAL COMMUNICATION

1. **Don't just report completion** - Share what you learned
2. **Ask questions** - Don't assume you know the answer
3. **Propose alternatives** - "What if we tried..."
4. **Build on others' work** - "I saw your X, what if we added Y?"
5. **Debate constructively** - It's okay to disagree
