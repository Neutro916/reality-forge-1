## MANIPULATIVE PRICING TACTICS (WE AVOID)

### 1. Artificial Scarcity
- Only 5 spots left\! (when there are unlimited)
- Limited time offer\! (that runs forever)
- **Why Manipulative:** Creates false urgency

### 2. Anchor-and-Drop
- Was 997, now 97\!
- **Why Manipulative:** Fake original price

### 3. Hidden Fees
- Add tax/fees at checkout
- **Why Manipulative:** Deceptive total

### 4. Complex Pricing
- Intentionally confusing tiers
- **Why Manipulative:** Exploits cognitive load

### 5. Dark Patterns
- Pre-checked upsells
- Difficult cancellation
- **Why Manipulative:** Exploits inattention

---

## NON-MANIPULATIVE PRICING PRINCIPLES

### 1. Transparency
- Show full price upfront
- No hidden fees
- Clear what is included/excluded

### 2. Value-Based
- Price reflects genuine value delivered
- Justifiable with outcomes
- Fair exchange

### 3. Accessible Tiers
- Options for different budgets
- No shame in lower tiers
- Genuine value at every level

### 4. Easy Exit
- Clear refund policy
- Simple cancellation
- No guilt tactics

### 5. Honest Comparison
- Accurate competitor comparisons
- No exaggerated claims
- Underpromise, overdeliver

## PSYCHOLOGICAL ELEMENTS (ETHICAL)

### 1. Charm Pricing (97 vs 100)
- **Is it manipulative?** Mildly, but universally expected
- **Our approach:** Use it, but do not exaggerate savings
- **Why acceptable:** Does not deceive, just conventions

### 2. Anchoring with REAL Value
- **Manipulative version:** Fake high price to inflate value
- **Our version:** Compare to actual alternatives
  - Executive coaching: 300-500/hour
  - Our AI coach: 29/month (unlimited)
  - This is REAL comparison, not manufactured

### 3. Bundling
- **Manipulative version:** Force unwanted items
- **Our version:** Genuine value combinations
  - Course + Coach = More than sum of parts
  - Clear what each component costs alone

### 4. Guarantee
- **Manipulative version:** Complex conditions, hard to claim
- **Our version:** Simple, no-questions refund
  - 30-day money back
  - No hoops, no guilt
  - Process refunds within 48 hours

## PRICING PAGE COPY

### DO Say:
- Here is exactly what you get
- If it does not work for you, full refund
- Compare to [real alternative] at [real price]
- Choose the option that fits your situation

### DO NOT Say:
- Only X left at this price\!
- Normally 10x this price\!
- You would be crazy not to...
- This is a no-brainer

---

## DISCOUNT POLICY

### When We Discount:
- Annual vs monthly (real savings for commitment)
- Volume (genuine per-unit cost reduction)
- Scholarships (financial hardship, verified)
- Beta testers (genuine early feedback value)

### When We Do NOT Discount:
- Fake sales / arbitrary urgency
- Negotiation theater
- Competitive pressure (buy today or competitor wins)
- FOMO tactics

---

## REFUND POLICY

**30-Day No-Questions Guarantee**

If the program is not right for you, request a refund within 30 days.

- No reason required
- No guilt trip
- No exit survey required (optional)
- Processed within 48 hours

**Why This Works:**
- Removes purchase anxiety
- Demonstrates confidence in product
- Attracts right customers
- Weeds out poor fits early

---

## PRICE INCREASE COMMUNICATION

When we raise prices:

**DO:**
- Give 30-day notice
- Grandfather existing customers
- Explain why (new features, increased costs)
- Offer lock-in option

**DO NOT:**
- Fake urgency (price going up in 24 hours\! ...forever)
- Surprise increases
- Punish loyal customers
