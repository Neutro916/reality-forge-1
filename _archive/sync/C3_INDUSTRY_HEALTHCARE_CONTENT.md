## HEALTHCARE-SPECIFIC MANIPULATION PATTERNS

### Pattern 1: Authority Manipulation
"I'm the doctor, so my way is correct."
Using credentials to shut down valid concerns.

**Recognition:** Pulling rank instead of engaging with substance.
**Transformation:** Authority as responsibility, not weapon.

### Pattern 2: Fear-Based Compliance
"If you don't follow protocol, patients will die."
Using worst-case scenarios to control staff.

**Recognition:** Catastrophizing to prevent discussion.
**Transformation:** Evidence-based decision making with psychological safety.

### Pattern 3: Martyr Manipulation
"I stayed until 2 AM. Why didn't you?"
Using personal sacrifice to guilt others.

**Recognition:** Suffering as currency for control.
**Transformation:** Sustainable practice, not heroic burnout.

### Pattern 4: Information Hoarding
Keeping critical information to maintain power position.

**Recognition:** Knowledge as control mechanism.
**Transformation:** Open information flow, shared intelligence.

### Pattern 5: Hierarchy Weaponization
Using organizational structure to silence lower-level staff.

**Recognition:** "Chain of command" as silencing tool.
**Transformation:** Speak-up culture regardless of position.
