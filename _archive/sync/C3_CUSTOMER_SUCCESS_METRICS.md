## ENGAGEMENT METRICS

### E1: Login Frequency
**Definition:** How often do customers access the platform?
**Target:** 3+ logins per week during active program
**Why It Matters:** Engagement precedes transformation

### E2: Lesson Completion Rate
**Definition:** % of lessons completed
**Target:** 70%+ complete full program
**Why It Matters:** Content consumed = value delivered

### E3: AI Coach Usage
**Definition:** Conversations per user per month
**Target:** 4+ conversations/month
**Why It Matters:** Coaching drives application

### E4: Time in Platform
**Definition:** Average session duration
**Target:** 20+ minutes per session
**Why It Matters:** Depth of engagement

### E5: Exercise Completion
**Definition:** % of exercises completed (not just viewed)
**Target:** 50%+ exercises completed
**Why It Matters:** Exercises drive transformation

---

## PROGRESS METRICS

### P1: Module Progression Rate
**Definition:** Time to complete each module
**Target:** 1 module per week average
**Why It Matters:** Steady progress predicts completion

### P2: Drop-off Points
**Definition:** Where do customers stop?
**Target:** No module >20% drop-off
**Why It Matters:** Identifies content problems

### P3: Completion Rate
**Definition:** % who finish entire program
**Target:** 60%+
**Why It Matters:** Only completers transform

### P4: Time to Completion
**Definition:** Days from start to finish
**Target:** 30-60 days
**Why It Matters:** Momentum matters

### P5: Re-engagement Rate
**Definition:** % who return after 7+ day absence
**Target:** 40%+ of inactive users return
**Why It Matters:** Recovery signals program value

---

## TRANSFORMATION METRICS

### T1: Pre/Post Assessment Score Change
**Definition:** Change in Manipulation Quotient
**Target:** -10 point average reduction
**Why It Matters:** Direct measure of pattern change

### T2: Domain Score Improvement
**Definition:** Improvement in highest-scoring domain
**Target:** 30%+ reduction in blind spot domain
**Why It Matters:** Targeted transformation

### T3: Self-Reported Behavior Change
**Definition:** Survey: "Have you changed specific behaviors?"
**Target:** 80%+ report yes
**Why It Matters:** Conscious change is real change

### T4: Team Feedback Change
**Definition:** 360 feedback or team survey changes
**Target:** Measurable improvement reported
**Why It Matters:** Others notice the change

### T5: Net Promoter Score (NPS)
**Definition:** Would you recommend this?
**Target:** NPS 50+
**Why It Matters:** Satisfied customers refer

---

## BUSINESS METRICS

### B1: Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
**Definition:** Subscription revenue per month
**Target:** Growth month over month
**Why It Matters:** Business sustainability

### B2: Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
**Definition:** Cost to acquire a customer
**Target:** CAC < 1/3 of LTV
**Why It Matters:** Efficient growth

### B3: Lifetime Value (LTV)
**Definition:** Total revenue per customer
**Target:** $200+ average
**Why It Matters:** Customer relationship value

### B4: Churn Rate
**Definition:** % of subscribers who cancel
**Target:** <5% monthly
**Why It Matters:** Retention = recurring revenue

### B5: Refund Rate
**Definition:** % who request refund
**Target:** <5%
**Why It Matters:** Product-market fit indicator

### B6: Conversion Rate
**Definition:** % of leads who purchase
**Target:** Assessment to purchase: 5%+
**Why It Matters:** Funnel efficiency

## MEASUREMENT METHODS

### Automated Tracking:
- Login frequency (platform analytics)
- Lesson completion (LMS tracking)
- Time in platform (session tracking)
- AI Coach usage (conversation logs)

### Surveys:
- Post-module feedback (after each module)
- Program completion survey (at finish)
- 90-day follow-up survey
- Annual check-in

### Assessments:
- Initial assessment (onboarding)
- Final assessment (program end)
- Optional 90-day re-assessment

## HEALTH INDICATORS

### Green (Healthy):
- Engagement metrics meeting targets
- Completion rate >60%
- NPS >50
- Churn <5%
- Pre/post improvement >10 points

### Yellow (Watch):
- Engagement declining
- Completion 40-60%
- NPS 30-50
- Churn 5-10%
- Pre/post improvement 5-10 points

### Red (Urgent):
- Low engagement
- Completion <40%
- NPS <30
- Churn >10%
- Pre/post improvement <5 points
