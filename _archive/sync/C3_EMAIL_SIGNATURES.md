## SIGNATURE ELEMENTS

### Required:
- Name
- Company name
- One link (website or assessment)

### Optional:
- Title
- Phone number
- Social links
- Tagline
- Promotion
- Quote/testimonial

### Avoid:
- Multiple CTAs (pick one)
- Large images (delivery issues)
- Too many links (overwhelming)
- Quotes without attribution
- Outdated promotions

- Test on mobile
- Avoid images when possible
