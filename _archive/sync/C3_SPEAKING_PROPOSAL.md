## TALK TITLES AND DESCRIPTIONS

### Talk 1: Keynote

**Title:** The Manipulation Epidemic: Why Leadership Training Is Making It Worse

**Description (100 words):**
68% of employees do not trust their leaders. Despite billions spent on leadership development, the trust gap is widening. Why? Because most leadership training teaches manipulation tactics disguised as influence. In this provocative keynote, [NAME] reveals the seven domains where manipulation shows up in leadership and why even well-intentioned leaders fall into these patterns. Drawing on Pattern Theory and real transformation stories, attendees will learn to recognize manipulation in themselves and others - and discover what actually builds trust. This talk will change how you think about influence forever.

**Key Takeaways:**
- The difference between influence and manipulation
- The 7 domains where manipulation shows up
- Why common leadership tactics backfire
- What actually builds trust

**Ideal Audience:** Leadership conferences, executive retreats, management summits

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes

### Talk 2: Workshop

**Title:** Discover Your Blind Spots: A Pattern Theory Workshop

**Description:**
In this interactive workshop, participants will assess their own manipulation patterns using the Seven Domains framework. Through guided exercises and peer discussion, leaders will identify their blind spots, understand the impact on their teams, and develop a personal action plan for change. This is not theory - it is transformation.

**Format:**
- Assessment completion (15 min)
- Results interpretation (15 min)
- Domain deep dives (45 min)
- Peer coaching pairs (30 min)
- Action planning (15 min)

**Key Takeaways:**
- Personal manipulation quotient score
- Identification of primary blind spot
- Understanding of seven domains
- Concrete action plan

**Ideal Audience:** Leadership development programs, team offsites, HR conferences

**Duration:** 2-3 hours
