## INTERVIEW QUESTIONS FOR CASE STUDIES

### Background:
1. Tell me about your role and responsibilities.
2. How would you describe your leadership style before?

### Problem:
3. What challenges were you facing with your team?
4. What made you realize something needed to change?
5. How was this affecting your work and life?

### Discovery:
6. How did you find Pattern Theory?
7. What was your initial reaction to the assessment?
8. What surprised you most about your results?

### Process:
9. Walk me through your experience with the program.
10. What was the most challenging part?
11. When did you first notice a shift?

### Results:
12. What specific changes have you seen?
13. How has your team responded?
14. What metrics have improved?

### Reflection:
15. What would you tell your past self?
16. Who would you recommend this to?
17. What advice do you have for other leaders?
