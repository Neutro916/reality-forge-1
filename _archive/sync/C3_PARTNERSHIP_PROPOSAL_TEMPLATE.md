## PROPOSAL CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

### Before Sending:

1. Research the partner thoroughly
2. Identify specific pain points or goals
3. Customize value proposition to their needs
4. Select appropriate partnership type
5. Adjust business terms for their scale/context
6. Add partner-specific examples or case studies

### Key Elements to Customize:

- Partner name throughout
- Specific value proposition
- Business terms (revenue share, pricing)
- Integration specifics
- Timeline expectations
- Case studies/examples relevant to their space

### Red Flags (Don't Partner If):

- Values misalignment (manipulative practices)
- Unrealistic expectations
- No clear mutual benefit
- Competing directly with core offering
- Reputation risk
